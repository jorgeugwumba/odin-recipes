Paella

Description

Paella is a the name of a famous Spanish rice dish that is traditionally cooked in a paella pan. Paella typically includes Paella rice and a variety of ingredients such as seafood.

Ingredients

  • Paella Rice.
  • Shrimp, Mussels,etc.
  • Diced Onions.
  • Bell Peppers (Red and Green).
  • Garlic (minced).
  • Diced Tomatoes.
  • Smoked of Paprika.
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth (Use water if unavailable).
  • Olive Oil (For sautéing).
  • Salt and Pepper to taste.
  • Lemon Wedges and fresh parsley for garnish (optional).
  1. In a large, shallow pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onions and bell peppers and sauté until they soften, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add the paella rice to the pan and stir to coat it with the oil and vegetables. Sprinkle the paprika over the rice and stir to evenly distribute.
  3. Pour the chicken or vegetable broth (or water) into the pan and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and cook for a few minutes until they start to break down.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer the paella for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed. Check and stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Carefully arrange the mixed seafood on top of the rice. Push the seafood down into the rice mixture slightly but do not stir after this point.
  7. Cover the pan again and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
  8. Once done, remove the paella from the heat and let it rest for about 5 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld. If you have lemon wedges and fresh parsley, garnish the paella before serving.
