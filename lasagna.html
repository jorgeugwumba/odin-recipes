Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, Tomato sauce, and plenty of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta.
  • Grounded Beef.
  • Tomato Paste.
  • Mozzarella Cheese.
  • Ricotta Cheese.
  • Parmesan Cheese.
  • Egg.
  • Dried Basil.
  • Salt and Pepper.
  • Parsley,for garnish (optional).

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground meat over medium-high heat until it's browned. Break it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Drain any excess fat from the meat.
  4. Stir in the jar of pasta sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, dried basil or Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat and sauce mixture.
  7. Place three lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add another layer of three lasagna noodles.
  10. Spread the remaining cheese mixture over the noodles.
  11. Add a final layer of three lasagna noodles.
  12. Pour the remaining meat and sauce mixture over the top.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  14. Bake for about 25-30 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese over the top.
  16. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  17. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before slicing.
  18. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired, and serve hot.
